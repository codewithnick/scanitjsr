About Us

About Us

Society of Computer Applications of National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur was established on 6th February, 2016 in the premises of the Department of Computer Applications, with the vision of organising social and professional activities and events which will help in enriching ,fortifying and strengthening the community within the Department.

For The Students

A Happy life is one spent in learning and yearning. Our aim is to reciprocate it “For the Students”.

By The Students

Knowledge is Power. Education is premise of progress when it is brought up “By the Students”.

Of The Students

Our success is no accident. It is perseverance, sacrifice and most of all, the Pure love “Of the Students”.

What We Do

The Goal of the society is to organise social and professional activity which will strengthen and enrich
the community within the department and help to publicate various research activities within the department.
The core purpose will be to foster technological innovation and excellence.
